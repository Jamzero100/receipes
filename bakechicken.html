Bake Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • thyme
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • oil spray

Steps

  • Spray chicken with oil spray
  • Add dry seasoning to chicken and mix
  • Set oven to 425f and insert chicken in oven
  • Flip chicken at 30 minute mark and leave to cook for another 30 minutes
  • Remove from oven and allow to cool
  • Serve with a carb source and vegetables
