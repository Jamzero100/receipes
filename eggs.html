Scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • pinch of salt
  • black pepper
  • milk(optional)
  • cheese(optional)

Steps

  • Add butter to a heated pan
  • Add mixed eggs and ingredients to pan
  • Stir mixture in pan as egg begin to solidify
  • Once eggs are fully cooked add(cheese to eggs as well as any other toppings)
  • Serve with toast or any other carb source
