Pizza

Ingredients

  • tomato sauce
  • tortilla or pita bread
  • shredded mozzarella cheese
  • shredded chicken or other meat topping
  • bell peppers or option veg topping

Steps

  • Add 2 tbsp of tomato sauce to pita bread or tortilla
  • Add 100g of shredded chicken or meat topping
  • Add 50 grams of veg topping
  • Add 60 grams of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Set oven to 250F for 10 min
  • Remove and allow to cool
